The name of the CSV files contains information about:
- mouse ID: 2 digits
- date when the recording was done in the format: DDMMYY (6 digits)
- length of session in minutes: 2 digits 
- cumulative session number: 2 digits

An example is shown below:

05_061221_20_03.csv

Mouse with ID 05, 
Date of recording the 6th (06) of December (12) of 2021 (21), 
20 minutes of training, 
Session number 03
